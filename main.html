The Internet: A Computational Marvel

Introduction

The internet was invented with the creation of the ARPAN( Advanced Research Project Agency Network) funded by DoD It started by a network called Arpanet which linked mainframes at universities, government contractors, and such Later, the innovation transformed into a global network connecting computers, allowing user communication and serving as a information hub Nowadays, it is a network used for entertainment and work which attributed to the creations of multiple business sectors

Featherly, Kevin . “ARPANET | Definition & History.” Encyclopædia Britannica, 11 May 2016, www.britannica.com/topic/ARPANET.

Reason for Creation

The internet was a way for government researchers to share information, but it began as a research project at a military defense arm in the 1960s during the Cold war Interestingly, the first internet browser was invented by Tim Berners Lee in the 1990-1991.

Time line

  • 1969: The ARPANET went live with its first connection between UCLA and Stanford RI
  • 1983: The TCP/IP protocol was introduced, allowing different networks to connect
  • 1990-91: The World Wide Web (WWW) was invented
  • 1990s: Commercialization begins
    • Pizza Hut did the first e-commerce transaction in August of 1994 as they sold pizzas online
    • HTTP and HTML are invented
    • Amazon and eBay launch in the mid-90s
    • The introduction of broadband made internet access faster and more reliable
  • 2000s: DotCom Bubble
    • Several new websites, communication platforms, and entertainment methods are launched in the 2000s
      • Wikipedia
      • Facebook
      • YouTube
      • iPhone
      • Bitcoin
